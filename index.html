Título:**** FLUVSIM: a program for object-based stochastic modeling of fluvial depositional systems - Deutscha and Tranb

Expositor: Rafael Sanabria**********************

Data: Sexta-feira, 26 de setembro de 2014, 9:00***

Local: Sala 856*********************************

Resumo:***************************************

This paper presents a FORTRAN program for hierarchical object-based modeling of complex fluvial facies. Unique features of this program include (1) a simple approach to place channel, levee, and crevasse sands within a matrix of floodplain shales, (2) templates for fast rasteringof fluvial facies objects, leadingto fast CPU times, and (3) the use of simulated annealingand non-random perturbation rules for conditioningto extensive soft facies-proportion data and local well data. Object-based modelingtechniques are widely applicable to modelingfluvial depositional systems. Public domain software for such modelingis rare and inflexible with respect to the variety of conditioningdata that can be handled. Commercial software is costly and also of limited flexibility. The fluvsim program overcomes many of these limitations with an accessible research code.
